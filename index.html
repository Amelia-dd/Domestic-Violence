Domestic Violence

What is domestic violence

Domestic violence is defined as the violent or aggressive behavior within the home, typically involving the violent abuse of a a child,spouse or partner.

Signs of an abisive relationship

There are many signs of an abusive relationship,fear of your spouse is one of the most telling indication. If you feel like you have to carefully watch what you say and do to prevent a fight, your relationship is probably toxic and abusive. To help determine if you are in an abusive relationship please fill out the questionnaire below . If your answer is "yes "to most of the questions,there is a great possibility you're in an abusive relationship.
